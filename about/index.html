About Me

I am Tarashish Mishra, 18. I live in Jatni, a small town near Bhubaneswar in Orissa, India.

I am doing my B.Tech. in Computer Science in CET, Bhubaneswar. Being interested in the web technologies since the time I got my first computer ( at the age of 15 I think) I am learning stuff on my own. So far the journey has been quite fun thanks to the web and lovely people who contribute to it.( I love Bucky Roberts, Khan Academy, MIT OCW, Udacity, Stack Overflow Community for what they have taught me. Thanks a lot :) )

Other than that I still enjoy watching quality cartoons, eating home cooked food, drinking lots of coffee and playing Battlefield Heroes,an awesome cartoon-style multiplayer shooter.

I enjoy learning new things. I have tremendous interest in entrepreneurship, coding, traveling and meeting new people. If you want to do a fun project with me or have a casual little chat, feel free to contact me on Facebook or Twitter.

Some cool stuff I’ve done includes :

  • TweetDive - A simple twitter search app built using JQuery and Twitter search API.

  • InstaGreat - A Instagram Photo Gallery made with JQuery and the Instagram API

  • Things-To-Do - A to-do list app built using Rails.

  • Watchman - A python script that can log various data like the weather, websites you visit, songs you listen to. It saves the data with a screenshot of your computer screen. Supports plug-ins too.

This list might not be up-to-date all the time. To get up-to-date info see my Github and Bitbucket account. Or you can just contact me. I would love to collaborate :)
